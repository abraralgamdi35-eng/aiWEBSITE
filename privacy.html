Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

NEXUS AI collects minimal information:

  • Chat History: Stored locally on your device
  • Chat Codes: Unique identifiers for each chat session
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect emails, names, or any personal information

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the AI chat service
  • To remember your conversation history
  • To enable sharing via chat codes

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any server except:

  • Groq API: Your messages are sent to Groq for AI processing
  • SearXNG: For web search functionality

4. Security

  • All chats are identified by unique 6-character codes
  • Your chat history is only accessible on your device
  • No personal information is ever stored or transmitted

5. Your Rights

  • Export your chat history
  • Delete individual chats
  • Clear all data from your browser
